Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

1. Introduction

SaudiScore ("we", "our", or "the app") is a football scores app for iOS and Android. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

  • Anonymous user ID (generated automatically via Firebase Authentication)
  • Push notification token (FCM) for delivering match alerts
  • Your followed teams, leagues, and notification preferences (stored in Firebase)
  • Basic usage data via Firebase (crash reports, performance)

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver push notifications for matches you follow or pin
  • To save your preferences across sessions and devices
  • To improve app stability via anonymous crash reporting

4. Third-Party Services

SaudiScore uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, push notifications) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • API-Football (football data provider) — api-football.com

5. Data Retention

Your preferences and notification settings are stored as long as you use the app. You can contact us to request deletion of your data.

6. Children

SaudiScore is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
